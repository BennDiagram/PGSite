OUR MISSION

Post Genre emerged from Oakland’s DIY music scene in 2022 with a clear mission: to create a permanent home for the arts in Oakland. Over the past two years, we’ve had the privilege of building a network of musicians, organizers, and creatives who share a common goal: to bring people together through live arts. 

JOIN THE TEAM

Our team includes musicians, artists, sound/lighting engineers, event organizers, photographers, videographers, editors, writers. If you're passionate and willing to learn, you'll fit right in!

HAVEN

Haven is an independent performance space in the heart of Pittsburgh’s Oakland neighborhood. Haven serves as a creative home where musicians, artists, and audiences can come together for concerts and events in a secure and professional environment. 

PG STUDIOS

Starting this January, PG Studios will offer a smart rehearsal space in Oakland that will provide musicians with a convenient, professional, and affordable environment to rehearse, record, and create. PG Studios is equipped with backline to record rehearsals, live sessions, create content, and shoot photos. The 2500 sq ft church will also be available to rent for private events.
