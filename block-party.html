2025 OAKLAND BLOCK PARTY

ABOUT THE EVENT

The 2025 Oakland Block Party is a free outdoor music festival featuring performances from eight local bands and a wide range of vendors. The event takes place on Wednesday, August 20th on Schenley Drive right across from the Cathedral of Learning. The festival will welcome thousands of students as they return to Oakland for the academic year.

Schenley Drive will be closed to traffic and turned into a walkable festival space with room for up to 50 local vendors and hundreds of attendees. A 20’x16’ stage will be set up at the lower end of the street, where bands will perform throughout the day.
